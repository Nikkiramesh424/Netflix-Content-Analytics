Netflix Content Analytics Project

Analysis 1: Movies vs TV Shows

Total Records: 8807

Movies: 6131
TV Shows: 2676

Insights:
1. Movies represent the majority of Netflix content.
2. Approximately 70% of content consists of Movies.
3. Approximately 30% of content consists of TV Shows.

Analysis 2: Top Countries Producing Netflix Content

United States: 2818
India: 972
United Kingdom: 419
Japan: 245
South Korea: 199

Insights:
1. The United States contributes the highest amount of content on Netflix.
2. India is the second-largest content producer.
3. The top two countries contribute significantly more content than the remaining countries.
4. Netflix content is heavily concentrated in a few major production regions.

Analysis 3: Content Release Trend

2018 had the highest number of releases with 1147 titles.

Insights:
1. Netflix content production increased rapidly from 2014 to 2018.
2. 2018 was the peak year for content releases.
3. Content production remained high during 2019 and 2020.
4. The platform experienced significant growth between 2015 and 2018.

Analysis 4: Content Ratings

Top Ratings:
TV-MA = 3207
TV-14 = 2160
TV-PG = 863

Insights:
1. TV-MA is the most common content rating on Netflix.
2. The platform primarily targets mature audiences.
3. Teen and adult-oriented content dominates the catalog.
4. Family-oriented content represents a smaller share of total titles.

Analysis 5: Top Genres

Top Genres:
1. Dramas, International Movies
2. Documentaries
3. Stand-Up Comedy

Insights:
1. Drama is one of the most dominant content categories.
2. International content plays a major role in Netflix's library.
3. Documentaries are among the most common content types.
4. Netflix offers a diverse mix of entertainment genres.
